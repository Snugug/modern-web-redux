# Article
A type of [Article](http://schema.org/Article)

Short to long form text with possible accompanying images of recent factual stories

## Benefits and Value

As a *Political Junkie*, I want *the most up-to-date information about the state of the world* so that *I stay an informed citizen*

Value to *Political Junkie*: **13**


As a *Video Journalist*, I want *to relate my videos to a news article* so that *my viewers have context*

Value to *Political Junkie*: **3**


As a *Company Executive*, I want *to increase traffic to our site during peak news stories* so that *we enhance our standing as a world leading news source*

Value to *Political Junkie*: **5**
